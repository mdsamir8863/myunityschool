About

Unity Phool Bun

Unity Phool Bun is a preschool under the umbrella of Touhid Education Society, focusing on early childhood education. Established with the goal of nurturing young minds, the school provides a warm and engaging environment for children to grow and learn.

Our approach revolves around play-based learning, character building, and social development, ensuring that children are well-prepared for the next stages of their education.

Learning Approach

Play-Based Learning

At Unity Phool Bun, we emphasize play-based learning to stimulate a child’s imagination, curiosity, and social skills. Our curriculum includes interactive activities that develop motor skills, language abilities, and problem-solving skills through fun and engaging methods.

The creative curriculum promotes hands-on experiences, allowing children to learn in a joyful and supportive environment.

Facilities

Child-Friendly Environment

Unity Phool Bun provides a safe and welcoming environment for young children, equipped with child-friendly furniture, toys, and learning materials. Our classrooms and outdoor play areas are designed to support both structured learning and free play, enabling children to explore and grow confidently.

Our school ensures that all facilities meet safety standards, creating a secure atmosphere where parents can confidently entrust their children’s early education.
